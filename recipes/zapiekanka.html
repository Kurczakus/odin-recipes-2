Zapiekanka

The simple polish meal that storm peoples hearts

The king of midnight and summer breaks. Simple yet tastefull. Polish zapiekanka is a street food that you will love and will craving for.

list of ingredients:

  • 1 baguette
  • butter
  • Gouda cheese
  • mushrooms (ideally champignons)
  • ketchup
  • salt and pepper
  • ham or any meat of your choice

How to prepare this amazing meal

  1. Cut the baguette a half and spread the butter on it
  2. Fry the mushrooms in a pan with small amount of oild, pepper and salt
  3. Put mushrooms on a baguette and grated chees on top of that
  4. Add ham/meat
  5. Put your zapiekanka into oven for 180 degrees for 10 min. Your baguette should be crispy and gold, and cheese melted
  6. Take out your zapiekanka from the oven and add some ketchup on top of it
  7. take a phote and sent to all your friends.
